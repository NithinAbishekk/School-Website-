Introduction to Biodiversity


The term biodiversity was coined in 1985.Biodiversity includes the number of different organisms and their relative frequencies in an ecosystem. It also reflects the organization of organisms at different levels.It provides us with nourishment, housing, fuel, clothing and several other resources.






Introduction to Natural Habitats


A natural habitat is an ecological or environmental area where a specific species lives.
It is the place in nature that particular species calls home.Let's discuss some
of the different types of natural habitats and the species that reside in them.
