What is Natural Habitat ?


A natural habitat is an ecological or environmental area where a specific species lives. Every living thing has a particular place in which it lives, and we call this place its habitat. A good habitat provides everything an organism needs to survive. For some species it could be deep in a forest, while for others it could be deep in the ocean.

ㅤㅤㅤCombat Natural Habitat loss: (Protect Natural Habitats)

We can combat habitat loss by preserving natural resources and learning how to use them in a way that does not require such frequent destruction of habitats. We should also teach others the importance of biodiversity. Comprehensive education regarding the environment helps people to understand how necessary biodiversity is. As quickly as humans can destroy natural habitats, we should be just as willing to put forth the effort to rebuild and attempt to replace what is lost.
